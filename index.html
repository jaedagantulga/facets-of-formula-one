The Facets of Formula 1 in 2022

Formula 1's popularity has only increased as the international racing championship has progressed through the seasons. Not only are the cars fast but they travel all over the world to compete in a series of 23 Grand Prix's. Over the season, teams compete for the Driver's Championship and the Constructor's Championship as they accumulate points throughout the season.
